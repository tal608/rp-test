# Internal & External Linking Audit

## Current State Analysis

### ✅ Internal Linking - What We HAVE:
1. **Header Navigation** - Basic main pages
2. **Footer Quick Links** - Basic service links
3. **Main Grooming Page** - Links to city pages ✅
4. **City Pages** - Link back to main grooming page ✅
5. **Breadcrumbs** - On most pages ✅

### ❌ Internal Linking - What We're MISSING:
1. **Homepage → City Pages** - No direct links from homepage to location pages
2. **City Page Cross-Linking** - Cities don't link to each other (related locations)
3. **Service-to-Service Links** - Grooming page doesn't link to hiking, vice versa
4. **Specialty Pages** - Puppy/canine grooming pages not linked from main grooming page footer/resources
5. **Contextual Links in Content** - Article content doesn't have inline links to related pages
6. **Related Pages Sections** - No "You might also like" sections
7. **Footer Enhancement** - Footer could have more links to resource pages

### ✅ External Linking - What We HAVE:
1. **Social Media** - Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Yelp ✅
2. **Google Maps Directions** - GetDirectionsButton component ✅
3. **Yelp Business Page** - Linked ✅

### ❌ External Linking - What We're MISSING:
1. **Google Business Profile Direct Link** - No direct link to GBP (only maps directions)
2. **Review Links** - No "Leave a Review" buttons for Google or Yelp
3. **Directory Links** - No links to other directories (BBB, Yellow Pages, etc.) if they exist
4. **Local Partnerships** - No links to vet partners, pet stores, etc.

## Recommendations

### Priority 1: Internal Linking Improvements
1. Add homepage section linking to city pages
2. Add cross-links between city pages
3. Add service-to-service links
4. Add links to puppy/canine pages from relevant pages
5. Add contextual links within article content
6. Add "Related Pages" sections

### Priority 2: External Linking Improvements
1. Add Google Business Profile direct link
2. Add "Leave a Review" buttons/links
3. Add any directory links if available
